E. Substitution.

You will now·hear a model sentence followed by a cue
Use this cue to make your new sentence. Like this:

You will hear: nyangatja pa1ya/palatja
You will say: palatja palya.

Then you listen for the correct response and repeat
it. This is called a four phase drill, because of
the four items:

1. The model: nyangatja palya/palatja 
2. Your attempt: palatja palya
3. The confirmation: palatja palya
4. Your repetition: palatja palya

Join in as soon as you understand.
