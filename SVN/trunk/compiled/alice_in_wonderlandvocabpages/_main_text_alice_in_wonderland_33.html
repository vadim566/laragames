‘Fury said to a

mouse, That he

met in the

house,

“Let us

both go to

law: I will

prosecute

you. 🔊--Come,

I’ll take no

denial; We

must have a

trial: For

really this

morning I’ve

nothing

to do.” 🔊

Said the

mouse to the

cur, “Such

a trial,

dear Sir,

With

no jury

or judge,

would be

wasting

our

breath.” 🔊

“I’ll be

judge, I’ll

be jury,”

Said

cunning

old Fury:

“I’ll

try the

whole

cause,

and

condemn

you

to

death.”’ 🔊
