“Warlboonidhi” is also made of other, smaller pieces. “Ni” makes a ‘being’/‘existing’ verb out of “warlboo.” “Dhi” means the action is happening now. Sometimes, instead of “–ni” you’ll see “–rri” used. It works in the same way.

Minimal pair

A minimal pair is when there’s only one in a word that makes it different from another word. If you change the one sound, you get totally different words.

manggooroo “strong” banggooroo “weak”
wanha “which; what; where” warda “stump; stem”
walha “wild turkey” warna “belly”
yabma “back of a man” wabma “wise”

“Minawarlboo” memoriser: When you are asleep, your eyes fall down, they are weak.
