Nhoonyara igadha; gayabidni gooda badloodhoo 🔊

He will survive; he won’t die of the spear wound

Literally: “Surviving live”; spear (gaya) from (-bidni) not to die.

Did you notice?

Notice the difference between mooriri and mooriridhi?

In Barngarla, some action words have been made by adding dhi (meaning “to be”) to a describing word, making a new word.

You will see this happen again in the next wordlists.
